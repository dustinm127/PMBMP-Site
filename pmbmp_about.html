ABOUT

Dustin Grant is an international award-winning freelance filmmaker based in Dallas, Texas, and the owner of Pink Mouse Blue Mouse Productions.


He grew up on a small tree farm in rural Louisiana, an upbringing that shaped his relationship with landscape and fostered a strong, imaginative inner life. That foundation left him with a lasting draw to open spaces as well as to major cities. One reflects his roots; the other satisfies his need to observe, study, and understand people and culture.


Dustin spent two decades in the corporate technology world, building business-to-business platforms and learning alongside some of the most accomplished professionals in his field. After leaving corporate life to pursue creative work full-time, his artistic approach became increasingly influenced by a fascination with simplicity and order. He gravitates toward minimalism, believing that form and function must work in harmony for art to feel complete.


He believes one of the highest callings a person can pursue is an honest search for understanding, whether that takes the form of learning how to cook a new dish or exploring why art has the power to inspire people to greatness.


Over the past ten years, that pursuit has taken him around the world, including extended time in Iceland, Belize, Costa Rica, Mexico, the U.S. Virgin Islands, and frequent visits to U.S. national parks. His work spans writing and directing commercials and narrative films, developing science fiction novels, managing a nonprofit vegetable farm, and occasionally stepping behind the camera to capture still images.


The pursuit of understanding nearly everything consumes him, and he is comfortable with that. He believes it is important work.
